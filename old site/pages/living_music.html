Musical Life

An auditory experiment, linked to Conway's Game of Life.

Home

  • Click on the grid to manually add cells, or just hit Randomise and listen!
  • Vertical axis corresponds to pitch, horizontal axis is time.
  • Each playthrough is one generation of the living cells.
  • Cells die if they have fewer than two neighbours, or more than three.
  • Cells come alive if they have 3 neighbours.
  • Performance may vary depending on the browser you're using.
  • See also: Tribal Life, a more simple game of life implementation without the music (but with multiple possible tribes!)
